Welcome to the vibrant and electrifying world of Hip-Hop, where rhythm, poetry, and culture collide to create a powerful and dynamic art form. Originating in the streets of New York City in the 1970s, Hip-Hop has transcended its humble beginnings to become a global phenomenon that influences music, fashion, and lifestyle. This genre is more than just music; it's a movement that empowers, inspires, and connects people from all walks of life. Hip-Hop culture is built on four fundamental pillars: MCing (rapping), DJing (turntablism), breaking (dance), and graffiti art. Each pillar that defines Hip-Hop. Whether it's the rhythmic flow of a skilled MC, the intricate scratches of a DJ, the acrobatic moves of a breakdancer, or the vibrant visuals of graffiti, these pillars unite to create a holistic and immersive experience.
